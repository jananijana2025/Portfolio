My Story

Check out my bylines:

Janani is a graduate student with a specialization in data-focused investigative journalism from Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism in Chicago. She was born and raised in Bangalore, in southern India. She is interested in business-focused narrative journalism and longform investigations but does not want to limit herself to it. She is currently working with the city desk at Chicago Sun-Times for her Master's internship and also worked with the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel's investigations team.

Prior to her roles in Chicago, she was the New Delhi correspondent for an Indian broadcast channel, juggling video and web priorities as a financial policy jounralist. Her early career experiences range across print, broadcast, digital and radio, making her flexibility her biggest strength. She firmly believes that a nose for newsworthiness and the persistence that goes with being a reporter are transferable skills across continents.
