My Story

Janani is a graduate student specializing in investigative journlism at Northwestern University's Medill School of Journalism in Chicago. She was born and raised in Bangalore, in southern India, and has a strong affinity to her Indian roots. She is interested in narrative journalism and longform investigations but does not want to limit herself to it. Her past experience includes reporting on topics like financial policy, domestic taxation and bilateral relations, pertaining to the Global South and Southeast Asia. She hopes to work at the intersection of data and insight-driven narratives.

As a graduate student, she is currently externing with the investigations team at the Milwaukee Journal Sentinel. Her previous stints include positions in broadcast, radio, magazine and digital writing. Her most recent full-time stint ended right before grad-school at NDTV Profit, a broadcast business news channel in New Delhi as a policy correspondent.
